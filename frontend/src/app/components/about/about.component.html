Story About SankatMochan
Legal Solutions LLP

We are a multi-disciplinary firm of solicitors and advocates based in New Delhi, with a team of lawyers experienced in matrimonial laws, criminal laws, property laws, trust laws, business laws, intellectual property rights and company audit protocols.

We provide effective legal solutions and counsel to our clients, at the most affordable prices. The core practice area of our team is litigation in Trial Courts, Hon’ble High Courts and the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India as well as before various tribunals and other quasi judicial bodies.

We also have a team of advocates engaged in transactional legal services such as company and society registration, corporate compliance audits, competition law issues, the establishment and audit of trusts and wills, registration of marriages, patent and trademark registrations etc.

We also provide professional and customised drafting services for contracts, agreements, deeds, wills and other such contractual legal documents for individuals, partnerships, firms, companies and societies.

Our in-house subject matter experts in the area of technology and IPR laws can draft agreements and contracts for lease and assignment of IPR, as well as technology products or services, such as software contracts, e-commerce agreements, copyright lease, copyright transfers and other such allied legal documents.
